Required Materials:

-Microcontroller (any Arduino works, and almost any ESP32 does as well)

-Piezo buzzer

-PIR motion sensor (make sure it runs on a similar voltage as your microcontroller)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Optional Materials (but required/semi-required if you want to complete the build):

-LiPo battery with a protection circuit

-TP4057 charging/BMS module

-Cardboard box/3D Printer/any casing

-Buck/boost converter

-Soldering iron (also extra optional)

-Breadboard (also also extra optional)
